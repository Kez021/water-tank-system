Weekly Maintenance Tips

  1. Sensor Cleaning (Ultrasonic Sensor)

English: Gently wipe the sensor lens with a dry, lint-free cloth to remove moisture or dust buildup that may cause false readings.

Tagalog: Dahang-dahang punasan ang lente ng sensor gamit ang tuyo at malinis na tela para maalis ang hamog o alikabok na nagiging sanhi ng maling reading.

  2. Tank Sediment Check

English: Regularly check the inside of the tank for any accumulated dirt or sludge to ensure that the water your family uses remains clean and safe, while also protecting the pump from clogging or damage.

Tagalog: Regular na suriin ang loob ng tanke kung may naipong dumi o putik, upang masiguro na ang tubig na ginagamit ng iyong pamilya ay mananatiling malinis at ligtas, habang pinoprotektahan din ang pump para hindi ito mabara o masira.

  3. Control Box & Wiring

English: Ensure the control box is tightly sealed and free from ants or insects. Check for loose wire connections to prevent system timeouts.

Tagalog: Siguraduhing selyado ang control box at walang langgam o insekto sa loob. I-check kung may maluwag na wire para hindi mag-error ang system.

  4. Pump Observation

English: Listen for unusual vibrating sounds from the pump. Unusual noise may indicate air in the pipes or mechanical wear.

Tagalog: Pakinggan kung may kakaibang ingay ang pump. Ang sobrang ingay ay maaaring senyales na may hangin sa tubo o may sira na ang makina.
